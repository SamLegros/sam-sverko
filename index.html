A little bit about me

Hi, I'm Sam and I'm a web developer.

I'm a natural problem solver who is not afraid of a challenge and believe that being able to have fun on the job makes any project possible.

I'm a "big picture"​ thinker who is able to visualize problems before they happen, leading to balanced, complete, and responsive projects in inclusive design and web development. Web development is my specialty, but working with people, for people is what I do best.

I believe in working towards greater digital access for all, the passion and dedication of small teams, the unlimited potential of technology, and the power of a good night's sleep.

I'm a recent Master of Digital Media grad, specializing in accessibility in web development and have a BFA in New Media from Ryerson University and am currently based out of Toronto.

When I'm not working, you can find me cooking, reading, and playing games (HotS, Destiny 2, or Horizon: Zero Dawn ← definitely recommend), usually in that order.
